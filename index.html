Tafkheem

Linguistic Definition (Luga):

  • Magification
  • Opposite of Tarqeeq
    •

    Technical Definition:

    It is a term meaning to fatten a letter by making its articulation point fat, and in its characteristic strong. It is synonymous with thickening. Usage in laam and raa overpowered by it.
