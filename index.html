Tafkheem

Linguistic Definition (Luga)

  • Magnification
  • Opposite of Tarqeeq

Technical Definition (Listihalan)

It is a term meaning to fatten a latter my making it, in its makraj (articulation point), fat, and in its sifa, or characteristic, it is strong. It is synonymous with thickening. It overpowers its usage in laam and raa.
