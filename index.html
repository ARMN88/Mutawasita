Tafkheem & Tarqeeq

Tafkheem

Linguistic Definition (Luga):

  • Magification
  • Opposite of Tarqeeq
    •

    Technical Definition:

    It is a term meaning to fatten a letter by making its articulation point fat, and in its characteristic strong. It is synonymous with thickening. Usage in ل and ر overpowered by it.

    Letters of Isti'laa (Strongest to Weakest)

    • ط
    • ض
    • ص
    • ظ
    • ق
    • غ
    • خ

    Levels of Tafkheem

    1. A letter of Isti'laa that has a fatha (ـَ), and before it is an ʾalif (أ).
      • Example: ضَاقَ
    2. A letter of Isti'laa that has a fatha (ـَ).
      • Example: خَلَقَ
    3. A letter of Isti'laa that has a dumma (ـُ).
      • Example: طُبِعَ
    4. A letter of Isti'laa that has a kasra (ـِ).
      • Example: مَوَاخِرَ
    5. A letter of Isti'laa that's sakina takes the haraka of the letter before it.
      • Example: يَفْلِب (The ف has a sukun. The letter before it has a fatha (ـَ), so the level of Tafkheem for the ف is level 2.)
